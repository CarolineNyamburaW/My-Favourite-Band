THE MIGOS

Who Are The Migos

The Migos an American hip hop trio from Lawrenceville, Georgia, founded in 2008. They are composed of three rappers known by their stage names Quavo, Offset, and Takeoff. The three members are directly related and were raised together; Quavo is Takeoff's uncle, and Offset is Quavo's cousin.

Awards: In 2017 they worn the BET Award for Best Group and Best Collaboration Category.

In 2018 they again won the BET Awards for Best Duo/Group catergory.

They have a large fun base and are especially loved for their song which have good beats and production, simple yet catchy flows,singable choruses as well asjust being a fun listen. The fact that they are family and are always looking out for each other also goes a long way into making them more appealing to their funs.They are also loved for their elegant lifestyle from flashy cars and fashion are craved by most young folk.
